# Template Gallery

Use this page as the portfolio-level map of the template system.

## Template Families

| Template | Best For | Core Surface |
| --- | --- | --- |
| E-Commerce | marketplaces, DTC, shopping | catalog, cart, checkout |
| Social | content apps, communities | feed, stories, messages |
| News | media and editorial apps | article, categories, bookmarks |
| Fitness | habit, workout, wellness | activity, progress, achievements |
| Finance | banking, budgeting, fintech | dashboard, cards, transactions |
| Education | learning products | courses, quizzes, certificates |
| Food | ordering and delivery | restaurants, menus, orders |
| Travel | booking flows | flights, stays, itinerary |
| Music | streaming and podcasting | player, library, playlists |
| Productivity | work and personal systems | tasks, notes, focus, habits |

## How To Use This Gallery

- pick the closest template, not the perfect one
- reuse architecture and flows before redesigning everything
- compare two adjacent templates if your product spans categories
